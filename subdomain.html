Free Subdomain Program

Support the server and claim your free subdomain

How to Get a Subdomain

You can qualify for a free subdomain in the following ways:

  • Invite at least 5 active members to the server.
  • Boost the server with Discord Nitro.
  • Pay a one-time fee of $3 for lifetime access to your subdomain.

Eligibility Requirements

To maintain access to your subdomain, the following conditions apply:

  • You must remain an active member of the server.
  • Invited friends must stay active in the server. If your friends leave, your subdomain may be revoked.
  • If you unboost the server, your subdomain will be suspended until boosting is resumed.

What Happens If You No Longer Qualify?

If you lose eligibility, the following actions may occur:

  • Your subdomain will be suspended.
  • To reinstate access, you must re-invite friends or reboost the server.
  • The one-time $3 payment ensures lifetime access and is unaffected by eligibility changes.

Subdomain Rules & Usage

All subdomains must adhere to the server rules and Terms of Service. Misuse of subdomains, such as hosting prohibited content, will result in immediate suspension and potential server bans.

Need Help?

If you have questions about setting up your subdomain or maintaining eligibility, please contact a moderator or admin for assistance.
